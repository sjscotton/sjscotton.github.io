Every October, artists all over the world take on the Inktober drawing challenge by doing one ink drawing a day the entire month.

Inktober was created by Jake Parker in 2009 as a challenge to improve their inking skills and develop positive drawing habits. It has since grown into a worldwide endeavor with thousands of artists taking on the challenge every year. Check out the inktober homepage
