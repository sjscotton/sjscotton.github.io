About

Hello!

I’m Sarah Scotton. A software developer, artist, bartender, dog lover and craft beer enthusiast!

Born and raised in the pacific northwest, I’ve loved exploring all the majestic scenery this place has to offer. Which in turn helps fuel much of my artistic passion throughout my life.

For the greater part of my adult life I funneled my desire to create into a career bartending. More recently however I found a new passion in programming. This began with teaching myself Python to becoming a member of Cohort 11 at Ada Developers Academy. It's been an amazing life changing opportunity and I'm excited to see where the future takes me!
